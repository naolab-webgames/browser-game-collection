Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Naoto Lab ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Naolab Browser Game Collection website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

1.1 Local Storage Data

Our games use browser LocalStorage to save your game progress and high scores on your device. This data includes:

  • Game high scores (Memory Game, Whack-a-Mole)
  • Game statistics (Tic-Tac-Toe win/loss/draw records)

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can delete this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

1.2 Google AdSense Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Show personalized ads based on your interests
  • Measure ad performance and engagement
  • Prevent fraudulent activity

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our game services
  • To save your game progress and high scores locally
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To improve our website and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense for advertising. Google may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior across different websites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies (primarily through Google AdSense). Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide a better user experience and display relevant advertisements.

You can control and/or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our website.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, please note that:

  • Game data is stored locally on your device using browser LocalStorage
  • We do not collect or store personal information on our servers
  • We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the Internet

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored in your browser's LocalStorage
  • Delete your game data by clearing browser storage
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through Google's settings
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Form.
